About the Artist:

Eileen Finn


Eileen Finn has been an ESL teacher for 18 years in 6 different countries. Moreover, she is a published researcher who has presented her work at multiple international conferences, a professional painter featured at several galleries, a filmmaker and a children's author based in the Pacific Northwest. Eileen has decided to switch gears and try her chances as a coder. Follow her on her journey as she learns the tricks of the trade and joins other women in trying to break the glass ceiling of the tech industry.
